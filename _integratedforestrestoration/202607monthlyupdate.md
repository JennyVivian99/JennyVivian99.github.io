# 🌱⛅Monthly update: July 2026🌊🌴
Temperatures are high in the cities of the Boreal Hemisphere, while in the Austral one, winter is chilling the fur of animals and the skin of humans.
While being closed up in an office can help fight the heat waves or the freezing mornings, we must not forget that we belong to "the outside". Our cradle is what we usually call "nature", which, however, is nothing less than what surrounds us. Why are we turning our backs on what makes us feel alive?
Maybe because, at first, choosing the comfort of an indoor clean room seems the best option. But after a while, and above all, after trying, we know that nothing is better than spending time outside, coming back home with the "smell of the sun" on our skin.
In this month, once more, we should remember the beauty of forests, prairies and oceans...
