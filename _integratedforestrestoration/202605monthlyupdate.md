# 🌻🌿Monthly update: May 2026🌳🍂
It is always fascinating how complex Nature is, and sometimes we dive so deep into the processes occurring in the ecosystem that we forget the impact of the simple things.
Simple relationships, simple connections or processes. So obvious yet so difficult to see.
What about them? In the proposed readings and seminars of this month we may find out more about this simple complexity.
